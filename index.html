What is Don't Starve?

This is a guide about how to survive your first 20 days in Don't Starve. Don't Starve is a survival and high complex game in the survival category. Is one of the most challenging survival games so far and therefore is loved by many experimented gamers who want to test their limits. You can find more information about the game in their Steam Page.
